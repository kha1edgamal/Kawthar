About PSDSARC

The Prince Sultan Center for Defense Studies and Research is a center that aims to promote and support defense and security trends in Saudi Arabia by conducting and developing qualitative and technical research related to defense, security and strategy.

Center Goals

  • Conduct scientific and applied research related to the defense and security industries in the interests of Saudi Arabia
  • To carry out applied research to support technical capabilities and provide solutions. Conducting specialized scientific studies and consultations.
  • Contribute to the technical supervision of the technical transfer programs related to contracts for the purchase of equipment and weapons.
